who are we ?

Art is generally considered a creative product that does not embody the truth of culture in its flat connotation and its later fleeting dimension. It expresses man’s hopes, aspirations and aspirations, and more than that, it expresses the philosophy of his being in life, and accordingly it goes beyond being just a life necessity for a person like water and air to achieve a greater and more comprehensive meaning. From here, we, a group of drawing lovers, decided to create this site for those interested in this creative art to put in it some information, including the history of drawing, art galleries, historical paintings and educational topics for drawing, as we documented that with some drawings and videos.
